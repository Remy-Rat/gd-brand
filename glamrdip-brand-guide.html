Usage Notes

Headings

Use Extra Bold (800) or Bold (700) for headings. Sentence case preferred for a modern, approachable feel. Keep line heights tight at 1.1 - 1.2.

Body Copy

Regular (400) for body text at 16px minimum. Use Text Mid (#4A2D5E) for softer readability on light backgrounds. Line height of 1.6 - 1.7 for comfortable reading.

Product Labels

Bold or Extra Bold in uppercase with generous letter spacing (2 - 4px). White text on purple backgrounds for maximum contrast, consistent with packaging.

CTAs & Buttons

Semi Bold (600) at 14 - 16px. Purple Brand (#7B4FA0) as primary button colour with white text. Use Purple Deep (#5E2D82) for hover states.
